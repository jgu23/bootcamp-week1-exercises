the many species of Bird™ have fought for dominance. When the One Great Bird™ fell from the heavens into the mortal realm, that eldritch being dissolved into the effervescent Eagle, the devious Duck, the ferocious Falcon, the proud Penguin, the cynical Chicken, the opulent Owl, and many, many more, whose names have been lost to the ravages of times. Before Order and Chaos, before Time and Space, before Land and Sea, the Bird™ War has raged, at times threatening to tear the fabric of reality itself apart.

After the Fourth Great Calamity of the Phaethonic Period, the First Council of Birds™ convened, with a single representative from each of the Bird™ Species, and they turned their beaks to the skies, and called to the heavens for an answer, a solution to the struggle. And from the heavens came the response: that if the Birds™ could not decide for themselves by the next alignment of the stars, the world itself would end, and all Birds™ would be no more.

And now the alignment of the stars draws near. Facing this most grave of fates, the 78th convention of the Twelfth Council of Birds™ has issued a truce, and declared that the greatest Bird™ shall be determined amongst the current four most powerful species: the Chicken, the Duck, the Goose, and the Emu.

And so we gather here to vote upon which Bird™ shall reign supreme for the rest of Time.
